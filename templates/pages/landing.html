Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure with InvoiceFlow?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transfers and our servers are hosted in high-security data centers with 99.99% uptime guarantee. We also perform regular security audits to ensure your data stays protected.

Which payment gateways do you support?

We currently integrate with Paystack, Stripe, and PayPal. We also support direct bank transfers and local mobile money providers. Our system automatically reconciles payments from all these sources in real-time.

Can I use my own domain for invoices?

Yes! Our Pro and Enterprise plans allow you to host the client portal on your own subdomain (e.g., billing.yourcompany.com) for a seamless brand experience. This includes custom email sender addresses and branded PDF exports.
